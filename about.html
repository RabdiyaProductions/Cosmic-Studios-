About Cosmic Studios

The production arm powering BuildLogic, FlowForm, and future Rabdiya universes.

Studio mission

Cosmic Studios exists to take complex ideas – construction intelligence, wellness, legal strategy, kids' IP – and turn them into visual stories people actually care about.

We design worlds once and then reuse them across video, apps, merch, and live activations.

Core universes

  • BuildLogic Visual Lab
  • FlowForm Vitality
  • Cosmic Kids & Family
  • Legal & Justice storytelling
